Synopsis

On a bright sunny day, the interior of the car tends to heat up and get warm. This is because the car acts as a mini greenhouse, which traps infra-red radiation from the sun. The surface of the car tends to heat up first especially it is of dark and dull colour. In turn, the heated surface will set up a convection current, causing the interior of the car to get extremely warm. A common method employed to solve this issue is the sun shade. Normally put up while the car is stationary, the sun shade reflects the light away from the car interior, preventing the abovementioned situation. However, sometimes the owner may be in a hurry, and thus could not afford waste time putting up the sun shade. Another possible situation is whereby a user simply forgets about the sun shade. Thus, to solve this problem, I am proposing an automatic sun shade deployment system. During a sunny day, this system will automatically deploy the sun shade.

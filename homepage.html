Welcome to my CA2 Assignment website!

This website serves to document the works and progress of my electronic assignment.

The electronic assignment that I am doing is a concept prototype for a automatic sun shade depolyment system. A brief summary on how it works is that when the sun shines on the two LDRs on the circuit board, the microcontroller will send a signal to a servo motor to turn 90 degrees, effectively deploying the shade. When the sun sets, the microcontroller will send a signal to the servo motor to turn to 0 degrees, retracting the sun shade. The threshold analog input is set to a value high enough that the shade will not be deployed unless during a sunny day, preventing situations such as deployment of shade when the LDRs are placed under for example, a street light.
